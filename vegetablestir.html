Recipe: Vegetable Stir Fry

Estimated Calorie Intake: 200 kcal

Health Benefits

  • Rich in dietary fiber
  • Packed with vitamins and antioxidants
  • Low in fat and calories

Ingredients

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup carrot slices
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger

Instructions

  • Heat sesame oil in a pan or wok over medium heat.
  • Add garlic and ginger, saut√© until fragrant.
  • Add broccoli, bell peppers, and carrots; stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  • Drizzle soy sauce over the vegetables and mix well.
  • Cook for another 2 minutes, then serve hot.
