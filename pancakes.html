Recipe: Pancakes

Estimated Calorie Intake: 350 kcal

Health Benefits

  • Rich in carbohydrates for energy
  • Provides essential vitamins and minerals
  • Can include healthy toppings like fruits

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Butter (for frying)

Instructions

  • Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  • Beat the egg, then mix it with milk and add to the dry mixture.
  • Stir until the batter is smooth.
  • Heat a pan and add butter.
  • Pour the batter and cook each pancake for 2 minutes on each side.
  • Serve with your favorite toppings.
