Recipe: Smoothie Bowl

Estimated Calorie Intake: 250 kcal

Health Benefits

  • Rich in antioxidants from fruits
  • Provides essential vitamins and minerals
  • Boosts energy with natural sugars

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen berries
  • 1/2 cup yogurt or plant-based alternative
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Instructions

  • Blend the frozen berries, banana, and yogurt until smooth.
  • Pour the mixture into a bowl.
  • Top with granola, chia seeds, and fresh fruits.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy!
