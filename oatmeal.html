Recipe: Oatmeal

Estimated Calorie Intake: 200 kcal

Health Benefits

  • High in fiber for digestion
  • Good source of complex carbohydrates
  • Provides energy and promotes heart health

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups milk or water
  • 1 tsp honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fruits (e.g., banana, berries)
  • 1 tbsp nuts or seeds

Instructions

  • Bring milk or water to a boil in a saucepan.
  • Add the oats and reduce heat to simmer.
  • Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Pour into a bowl and top with fruits, nuts, and honey.
