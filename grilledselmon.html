Recipe: Grilled Salmon

Estimated Calorie Intake: 400 kcal

Health Benefits

  • Rich in omega-3 fatty acids
  • High-quality protein source
  • Promotes heart health

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon fillet
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  • Brush salmon with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  • Place the salmon on the grill and cook for 5-7 minutes per side.
  • Serve hot with your choice of vegetables or salad.
