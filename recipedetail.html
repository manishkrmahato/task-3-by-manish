Recipe: Spaghetti

Estimated Calorie Intake: 450 kcal

Health Benefits

  • Rich in carbohydrates for energy
  • Source of fiber if whole-grain spaghetti is used
  • Provides vitamins from the marinara sauce

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  • Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  • Add marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Toss cooked spaghetti into the sauce until evenly coated.
  • Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and Parmesan cheese.
