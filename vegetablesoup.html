Recipe: Vegetable Soup

Estimated Calorie Intake: 150 kcal

Health Benefits

  • Boosts immunity
  • Low-calorie and nutrient-rich
  • Supports digestion

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Heat vegetable broth in a pot over medium heat.
  • Add carrots, celery, and onions, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Stir in diced tomatoes and cook for another 5 minutes.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste, then serve warm.
